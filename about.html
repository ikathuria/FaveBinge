Watcher

What is Watcher?

Watcher is a web app that allows you to track what shows and movies you're watching, and what you've watched. It also recommends shows and movies based on your preferences for all the streaming services you have.

How does it work?

Watcher uses the your previous history to get information about movies and shows.

What's next?

Watcher is currently in development. I'm working on adding more features, such as a search bar, a way to add shows and movies to your watchlist, and a way to add shows and movies to your watched list.
